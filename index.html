Dr. Nick Marasagani

Dr. Nick Marasigan, a Harvard Medical Center graduate, recently established a cutting-edge medical practice in San Pablo, Laguna. In October 2022, the practice initiated operations with notable transactions: - Dr. Marasigan transferred P250,000 to the clinic's account. - Acquired a medical clinic and land, financed with P50,000 cash and a 5-year, 20% note payable. - Purchased medical equipment and supplies using a 24% note payable and cash. - Obtained a year-long "all-in-one" insurance plan for P20,000.

Bought P17,000 worth of medical supplies from San Pablo Supply. - Paid P73,000 in staff salaries. - Received P90,000 for research from the Laguna Experimental Drug Center. - Billed patients P317,000 and paid P23,000 for equipment repairs. - Collected P117,000 in cash from patients. October also saw additional financial activities, including bill payments, account purchases, patient collections, and a P200,000 cash withdrawal.
